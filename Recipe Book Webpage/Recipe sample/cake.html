Strawberry Chocolate Cake

A strawberry chocolate cake is a delightful dessert that combines rich chocolate flavor with the fresh, sweet taste of strawberries. It typically consists of layers of moist chocolate cake filled with fresh sliced strawberries and creamy chocolate frosting. The combination of chocolate and strawberries creates a perfect balance of sweetness and indulgence, making it a favorite treat for chocolate lovers and fruit enthusiasts alike. This decadent cake is often topped with additional strawberries or chocolate shavings for an extra touch of elegance and flavor.

Prepration time

  • Total:Approcimately 2.5 Hours
  • Prepration: 1.5 Hours
  • Cooking: 1.5 Hours

Ingredients

  • 1 and 3/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 3/4 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 2 cups granulated sugar
  • 1 and 1/2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1 and 1/2 teaspoons baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 cup whole milk
  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 1 cup boiling water
  • 2 cups fresh strawberries, sliced
  • 2 tablespoons granulated sugar
  • 1 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 3 and 1/2 cups powdered sugar
  • 1/2 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 4-6 tablespoons heavy cream or milk
  • Fresh strawberries, whole or sliced
  • Chocolate shavings or curls

Instruction

1.

Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease and flour two 9-inch round cake pans.

2.

In a large mixing bowl, sift together the flour, cocoa powder, sugar, baking powder, baking soda, and salt.

3.

Add eggs, milk, oil, and vanilla extract to the dry ingredients. Beat on medium speed for about 2 minutes until well combined.

4.

Stir in the boiling water, the batter will be thin. Pour the batter evenly into the prepared cake pans.

5.

Bake in the preheated oven for 30-35 minutes or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean. Allow the cakes to cool in the pans for 10 minutes, then transfer them to a wire rack to cool completely.

6.

While the cakes are cooling, prepare the strawberry filling by combining sliced strawberries and granulated sugar in a bowl. Let it sit for about 15-20 minutes to macerate.

7.

To make the chocolate frosting, beat the softened butter until creamy. Gradually add powdered sugar, cocoa powder, salt, vanilla extract, and heavy cream. Beat until smooth and creamy, adding more cream if needed for desired consistency.

8.

Once the cakes have cooled, place one layer on a serving plate or cake stand. Spread a layer of chocolate frosting over the top of the cake, then arrange the sliced strawberries on top.

9.

Place the second cake layer on top and frost the top and sides of the cake with the remaining chocolate frosting.

10.

Decorate the cake with additional strawberries and chocolate shavings if desired.

11.

Chill the cake in the refrigerator for about 30 minutes before serving to allow the frosting to set.

12.

Slice and serve your delicious strawberry chocolate cake and enjoy!
