Premium Delux Burger

The Supreme Deluxe Burger is a premium gourmet burger known for its exceptional taste and quality. It features a succulent beef patty grilled to perfection, topped with melted cheese, crisp lettuce, juicy tomatoes, onions, and tangy pickles, all sandwiched between a toasted brioche bun. Each bite offers a burst of flavor and satisfaction, making it a top choice for burger enthusiasts seeking an elevated dining experience.

Prepration time

  • Total:Approcimately 20 minutes
  • Prepration: 20 minutes
  • Cooking: 15 minutes

Ingredients

  • 1/2 pound of ground beef (preferably lean)
  • Salt and pepper for seasoning
  • 2 slices of cheddar cheese
  • 2 brioche burger buns
  • Lettuce leaves
  • Sliced tomatoes
  • Sliced onions
  • Pickle slices
  • Condiments such as ketchup, mustard, or mayonnaise (optional)
  • Butter or oil for toasting buns

Instruction

1.

Preheat your grill or a skillet over medium-high heat.

2.

Divide the ground beef into two equal portions and shape them into patties slightly larger than the size of your buns. Press a slight indentation in the center of each patty to prevent it from bulging during cooking. Season both sides of the patties generously with salt and pepper.

3.

Place the burger patties on the preheated grill or skillet and cook for about 3-4 minutes on each side for medium-rare, or adjust the cooking time according to your desired level of doneness. During the last minute of cooking, add a slice of cheddar cheese on top of each patty and allow it to melt.

4.

While the patties are cooking, split the brioche buns in half and lightly butter the insides. Toast them on the grill or in a skillet until golden brown and crispy.

5.

Once the burger patties are cooked to your liking and the cheese is melted, remove them from the heat and let them rest for a minute

6.

To assemble the burgers, place a lettuce leaf on the bottom half of each toasted bun, followed by a slice of tomato, a few slices of onion, and pickle slices.

7.

Carefully place a cooked burger patty with melted cheese on top of the vegetables.

7.

Spread your desired condiments on the top half of the toasted bun, then place it on top of the burger patty to complete the burger.

7.

Serve the premium deluxe burgers immediately with your favorite side dishes and enjoy!
