Sausage Pizza

Sausage pizza is a popular variation of the classic Italian dish, featuring a crispy crust topped with tomato sauce, melted cheese, and savory sausage. The sausage is typically seasoned with herbs and spices, adding a rich and flavorful element to the pizza. Additional toppings such as peppers, onions, or mushrooms may also be included to enhance the taste and texture. Sausage pizza is a favorite among pizza lovers for its hearty and satisfying combination of ingredients, making it a delicious choice for any meal or occasion.

Prepration time

  • Total:Approcimately 45 minutes
  • Prepration: 30 minutes
  • Cooking: 30 minutes

Ingredients

  • Pizza dough (store-bought or homemade)
  • Tomato sauce or pizza sauce
  • Mozzarella cheese, shredded or sliced
  • Italian sausage, either ground or sliced
  • Olive oil (for brushing the crust, optional)
  • Optional toppings such as sliced onions, bell peppers, mushrooms, or olives
  • Grated Parmesan cheese (optional, for sprinkling on top)
  • Italian seasoning or dried oregano (optional, for added flavor)

Instruction

1.

Preheat your oven to the temperature recommended for your pizza dough.

2.

Roll out your pizza dough on a floured surface and transfer it to a baking sheet or pizza pan.

3.

Spread pizza sauce evenly over the dough, leaving a small border for the crust.

4.

Sprinkle shredded mozzarella cheese over the sauce.

5.

Cook sausage in a skillet until fully cooked, then drain excess grease.

6.

Sprinkle cooked sausage evenly over the cheese.

7.

Add any optional toppings you like, such as sliced onions, bell peppers, or mushrooms.

7.

Bake the pizza in the preheated oven according to the dough's instructions, until the crust is golden brown and the cheese is melted and bubbly.

7.

Remove from the oven, let it cool for a few minutes, then slice and serve.
